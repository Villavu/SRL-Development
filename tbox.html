TBox.Invert¶

function TBox.Invert(Area: TBox): TBoxArray; constref;

Returns the boxes which surround Self in the given Area. Typically there is eight surroudning boxes:

  • Top Left

  • Mid Left

  • Bottom Left

  • Top Mid

  • Bottom Mid

  • Top Right

  • Mid Right

  • Bottom Right
